Intonio — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

Intonio (“the app”), developed by Casey Mullen (MullenSoft), is designed so that it collects, stores, and transmits no personal data. Everything happens on your device.

Data collection

Intonio does not collect any personal data. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. The app does not send any data to any server.

Microphone

Intonio requests microphone access to listen to your instrument and analyze pitch and intonation in real time. This audio is processed entirely on your device. Recent audio may be held briefly in memory during a practice session to power the scroll-back (“review”) feature; it is never written to permanent storage, never leaves your device, and is discarded when the session ends.

Third parties

Intonio shares no data with third parties, because it collects none.

Children

Intonio collects no data from anyone, including children.

Updates to this policy will be posted on this page.
