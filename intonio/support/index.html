Intonio — Support

Intonio is a real-time intonation monitor for violin and fiddle — it tracks one or two notes at once and shows your pitch and double-stop intervals on a treble staff.

Tips & FAQ

  • Grant microphone access when prompted. To enable it later: iOS Settings → Intonio → Microphone.
  • The app works fully offline; it needs no account and no internet connection.
